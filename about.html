Our Story

Crafting Exceptional Spaces Since 2011

Interior King Studio was founded with a singular vision: to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. What began as a small design consultancy has grown into a premier interior design studio known for its innovative approach and meticulous attention to detail.

Over the past 15 years, we've had the privilege of working with hundreds of clients, from homeowners seeking to create their dream spaces to corporations looking to inspire their teams through thoughtful design.

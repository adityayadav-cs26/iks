Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process?

Our design process begins with an initial consultation to understand your vision, followed by concept development, design presentation, revisions, and finally execution. We keep you involved at every step.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single room redesign may take 4-6 weeks, while a full home renovation can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Do you work with specific budgets?

Absolutely! We work with a wide range of budgets and will help you maximize your investment. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your budget and create a design plan that aligns with it.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes! We offer virtual consultations for clients who prefer remote meetings or are located outside our service area. We use video conferencing and digital tools to collaborate effectively.
